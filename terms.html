2.1 The hardware. Tyme Boxed is a physical NFC (Near Field Communication) device sold for a one-time price of ₹999 INR. The device is designed to work in conjunction with the Tyme Boxed mobile application (iOS or Android) to allow users to activate screen-time blocking profiles.

2.2 The applications. The Tyme Boxed mobile applications for iOS and Android are made available free of charge, indefinitely, to any user of a Tyme Boxed device.

iOS App

Android App

Supports email OTP or Google OAuth. Developed independently by Apps For Development from the ground up — no open-source projects, third-party SDKs, or external code libraries for its core functionality.
